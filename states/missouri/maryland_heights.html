Maryland Heights, Missouri: Suburban Energy with a Scenic Side

Trails, tunes, and tastes just outside St. Louis — Maryland Heights has more than meets the eye.

Maryland Heights by the Numbers

  • Population: ~28,000 (2024 estimate)
  • State: Missouri
  • Founded: Incorporated in 1985
  • Area: ~23.6 sq mi (61.1 km²)
  • Climate: Humid continental — hot summers, cold winters
  • Key Industries: Tech, hospitality, logistics, tourism
  • Notable Landmarks: Hollywood Casino Amphitheatre, Creve Coeur Lake, Aquaport
  • Transportation:
    • Major Roads: I-270, MO-364, MO-141
    • Nearest Airport: St. Louis Lambert (~10 minutes)
    • Transit: MetroBus, car-based city, trails for biking
  • Cultural Highlights: Live concerts, art events, multicultural dining

Cuisine: Global Eats with a Local Feel

Korean BBQ, Ethiopian platters, and everything in between — Maryland Heights has serious flavor.

Culture: Suburban, Sure — But Far from Boring

Music, hockey, community festivals — the cultural life here is anything but sleepy.

Coffee: Chill Spots with a Neighborhood Vibe
