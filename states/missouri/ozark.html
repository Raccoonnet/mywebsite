Ozark, Missouri: Where the River Bends and Life Slows Down

Old mills, fresh biscuits, and river trails — Ozark is small-town Missouri at its best.

Ozark by the Numbers

  • Population: ~22,400 (2024 estimate)
  • State: Missouri
  • Founded: Incorporated in 1890
  • Area: ~11.6 sq mi (30 km²)
  • Climate: Humid subtropical — hot summers, mild winters
  • Key Industries: Retail, construction, small business
  • Notable Landmarks: Ozark Mill, Finley River Park, Smallin Cave
  • Transportation:
    • Major Roads: US-65, MO-14
    • Nearest Airport: Springfield-Branson National (~25 mi)
    • Transit: Primarily car travel; walkable downtown
  • Cultural Highlights: Markets, concerts, antique shows, Finley Farms events
