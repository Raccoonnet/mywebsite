Sedalia by the Numbers

  • Population: ~21,900 (2024 estimate)
  • State: Missouri
  • Founded: 1860
  • Area: ~13.3 sq mi (34.5 km²)
  • Climate: Humid continental — hot summers, cold winters
  • Key Industries: Agriculture, manufacturing, logistics, tourism
  • Notable Landmarks: Missouri State Fairgrounds, Katy Depot, Daum Museum
  • Transportation:
    • Major Roads: US-50, MO-65
    • Rail: Amtrak service (Katy Depot)
    • Nearest Airport: Sedalia Regional; Kansas City Intl. (~90 mi)
  • Cultural Highlights: State Fair, Ragtime Festival, art events, parades
