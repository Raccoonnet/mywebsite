Des Peres, Missouri: Where Green Spaces Meet Grown-Up Calm

Suburban ease, rooted history, and parks that invite you to linger — Des Peres is a lifestyle, not just a location.

Des Peres by the Numbers

  • Population: ~9,200 (2024 estimate)
  • State: Missouri
  • Founded: 1934 (incorporated)
  • Area: ~4.3 sq mi (11.1 km²)
  • Climate: Humid continental — warm summers, cold winters
  • Key Industries: Healthcare, retail, professional services
  • Notable Landmarks: The Lodge, West County Center, Sugar Creek Park
  • Transportation:
    • Major Roads: I-270, Manchester Rd
    • Nearest Airport: St. Louis Lambert (~15 mi)
    • Transit: Car-centric, bike-friendly in parks
  • Cultural Highlights: Summer concerts, family festivals, historic walking tours
