St. Charles by the Numbers

  • Population: ~72,000 (2024 estimate)
  • State: Missouri
  • Founded: 1769
  • Area: ~25 sq mi (64.7 km²)
  • Climate: Humid continental — hot summers, cold winters
  • Key Industries: Tourism, healthcare, education, manufacturing
  • Notable Landmarks: Main Street, First Capitol, Katy Trail, Boat House Museum
  • Transportation:
    • Major Roads: I-70, MO-94
    • Airport: St. Louis Lambert Intl. (~10 mi)
  • Cultural Highlights: Festival of the Little Hills, Christmas Traditions, Arts District
