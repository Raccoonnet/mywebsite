Cottleville, Missouri: Where Small Town Feels New Again

Charming, friendly, and more connected than you’d expect — Cottleville is what suburban living looks like when it’s done right.

Cottleville by the Numbers

  • Population: ~6,200 (2024 estimate)
  • State: Missouri
  • Founded: 1839
  • Area: ~4.5 sq mi (11.6 km²)
  • Climate: Humid continental — four full seasons
  • Key Industries: Local retail, small business, education, construction
  • Notable Landmarks: Legacy Park, Historic Main Street, Frankie Martin’s Garden
  • Transportation:
    • Major Roads: MO-364, Mid Rivers Mall Dr
    • Nearest Airport: St. Louis Lambert (~18 mi)
    • Transit: Car-based, with walkable and golf-cart friendly core
  • Cultural Highlights: St. Pat’s Parade, music nights, town-wide events, art markets

Cuisine: Casual Dining, Local Vibe

Good food without the fuss — wood-fired pizza, hearty burgers, tacos on the patio, and drinks under twinkle lights. Simple, satisfying, and welcoming.

Culture: Small Town Traditions, Big Community Spirit

Festivals, concerts, and porch light gatherings — Cottleville's community spirit shows up in every season, every street, and every friendly wave.

Coffee: Morning Walks & Porch Conversations

Cozy cafés, familiar faces, and slow mornings — the coffee scene here is built on community, not caffeine trends.

Whether you're staying for a while or just passing through, Cottleville has a way of making you feel like you belong.
