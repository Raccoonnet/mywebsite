The Anheuser-Busch Brewery Tour

20 Dec 2025

If you’re a fan of beer, history, or just a good time, the Anheuser-Busch Brewery tour in St. Louis should definitely be on your list. I recently went on their Day Fresh tour, and it was an absolute blast from start to finish.

One of the first highlights? The famous Anheuser-Busch Clydesdales. Getting up close to these majestic horses in their stables was incredible—there’s something about seeing these gentle giants in person that photos just can’t capture.

The tour itself was about an hour and a half, and it covered everything from the brewing process to the packing and bottling lines. I loved seeing how a massive operation like this works behind the scenes, and the buildings on the campus are impressive, to say the least. The guides were friendly and shared fascinating tidbits about the history of Anheuser-Busch and its founders, which made the experience feel both educational and fun.

Now, for the best part: the samples. I got to try several beers, but the real standout was the “St. Louis Lager”, a vintage recipe that had been lost for decades. This exclusive brew is only available seasonally, and it was amazing to taste a piece of history that’s rarely seen outside the brewery. To top it off, they even gave us a freshly bottled beer to take home at the end of the tour—what a cool touch!

At just $15 for the tour, it was totally worth it. I’ve been on a few brewery tours before, but this one might just be my favorite. Between the historic vibes, the incredible marketing flair (looking at you, Clydesdales), and the tasting experience, it really felt like a perfect blend of fun and education.

If you find yourself in St. Louis and love a good beer story, the Anheuser-Busch Brewery tour is a must-visit. Cheers!
