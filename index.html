Welcome to Where My Map Leads

Mission Statement

Hi, I’m RaccoonNet — a traveler on a mission to experience all 50 states of the U.S., not just to pass through them, but to truly know them. This blog is my way of documenting that journey: the hidden gems, unforgettable locals, unexpected detours, and moments that turned a place into a memory.

When I started this adventure, I had no idea where to begin. That’s why this blog isn’t just for me — it’s for you too. Whether you’re dreaming up your own 50-state challenge, planning your next road trip, or just love learning about different parts of the country, I hope my experiences help guide and inspire you.

Let’s get to know America, one state at a time.
