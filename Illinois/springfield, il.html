Things I've Seen

OH-ICEE

OH-ICEE is a friendly shop that specializes in pastries and boba drinks. When I visited I purchased a Thai boba drink and an OH-ICEE Strawberry Banana crepe. Both were excellent, but the crepe was truly unique. The crepe was massive and turned out to be an excellent sharable treat.

To learn more about OH-ICEE, you can visit them at their website linked below.

OH-ICEE Official Website

Pease's at Bunn Gourmet

Pease's is a classy pastry and coffee shop. I tried both the coffee and the pastries. Both were good, but I was most impressed with their pastry work. The pastries that I tried were very elegant.

To learn more about Pease's, you can visit them at their website linked below.

Pease's at Bunn Gourmet Official Website

Grab-A-Java

Grab-A-Java is a local coffee chain in Springfield and sells excellent hand brewed coffee. I highly recommend their coffee and they have an excellent selection. Additionally, the staff was very friendly and accommodating. The only draw back is that their business is run from a drive through, and as such ordering is kind of slow. Still, it was the best local coffee that I had in Springfield.

To learn more about Grab-A-Java, you can visit them at their website linked below.

Grab-A-Java Official Website

Lincoln Tomb

The Lincoln Tomb was a timeless destination. It is definitely a must see if one ever travels to Springfield. The destination pays homage to one of our greatest presidents and has many statues and plagues describing his great work. The location is also very peaceful.

To learn more about the Lincoln Tomb, you can visit them at their website linked below.

Lincoln Tomb Official Webpage

Lincoln Memorial Garden and Nature Center

The Lincoln Memorial Garden and Nature Center is a wonderful location right near Lake Springfield to take in some nature and enjoy the Illinois outdoors. They have hiking and festivals sometimes. When I went, the fall festival was open and I had a good time listening to live music.

To learn more about the Lincoln Memorial Garden and Nature Center, you can visit them at their website linked below.

Lincoln Memorial Garden and Nature Center Official Website

Lake Springfield

A wonderful lake. It has fishing and boating.

Illinois State Fair

The Illinois State Fair is held in Springfield every year and offers many amazing activities for all ages. From rides to petting zoos, the Illinois State Fair has everything you could want with a state fair. I have been several times and I always have a fantastic time.

To learn more about the Illinois State Fair, you can visit them at their website linked below.
