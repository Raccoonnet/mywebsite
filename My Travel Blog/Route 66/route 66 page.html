US Route 66

State Information

US Route 66 is an old historic highway. It was one of the original highways and travels from Downtown Chicago, Illinois to Santa Monica Pier in Los Angeles, California.

To learn more about the historic US Route 66, you can visit the US Route 66 Wikipedia page.

US Route 66 Wikipedia

US Route 66 States

Illinois

The 21th state of the United States of America. The capital is Springfield and the state is known for it's megalopolis Chicago.

The route goes through the cities of Chicago, Dwight, Bloomington, Lincoln, Springfield, Litchfield, Edwardsville, and East St. Louis. There are of course more cities and towns that can be passed through, but these are the biggest.
