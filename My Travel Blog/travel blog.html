Arkansas

The 25th state of the United States of America. The capital is Little Rock and the state is known for it's natural wonders like mountains, lakes, and hot springs.

Colorado

The 38th state of the United States of America. The capital is Denver and the state is known for it's natural wonders like the Rocky Mountains.

Florida

The 27th state of the United States of America. The capital is Tallahassee and the state is known for it's natural wonders like the Everglades.

Illinois

The 21th state of the United States of America. The capital is Springfield and the state is known for it's megalopolis Chicago.

Indiana

The 19th state of the United States of America. The capital is Indianapolis and the state is known for it's racing arena the Indy 500.

Kentucky

The 15th state of the United States of America. The capital is Frankfort and the state is known for horse racing and alchohol such as bourbon and moonshine.

Missouri

The 24th state of the United States of America. The capital is Jefferson City and the state is known for it's city St. Louis and The Gateway Arch.

Ohio

The 17th state of the United States of America. The capital is Columbus and the state is known for it's city Cleveland.

Tennessee

The 16th state of the United States of America. The capital is Nashville and the state is known for it's country music and southern style cooking.

Wisconsin

The 30th state of the United States of America. The capital is Madison and the state is known for it's cheese, bratwurst, and beer.

Washington DC

The capital of the United States of America.

US Route 66 is an old historic highway. It was one of the original highways and travels from Downtown Chicago, Illinois to Santa Monica Pier in Los Angeles, California.
